Orphanage

You can help change a child life and future by providing education and care. Our orphan centers provide the children with the necessary shelter, healthy meals, clothes, healthcare and education to have a better life.s the well-being and the protection of the children are the main concerns at all times, volunteers are chosen very carefully and must be trained in working with children. A commitment of at least four weeks is mandatory for the orphanage volunteers
